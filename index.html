WHAT'S TRENDING NOW
Start your day with a coffee

Did you know that drinking coffee has many benefits? For example, it helps your mental health and helps you live longer. In addition, it reduces the occurrence of two types of diabetes. Coffee contains antioxidants and vitamins such as riboflavin, magnesium and potassium. And last but not least, did you know that coffee can help reduce depression? And believe us, our coffee will boost your mood!

Add sugar

I know I know! Sugar is not the best! But find out that it has some advantages, plus with our coffee... the perfect combination! It increases instant energy. The breakdown of sugar produces glucose, and as we know, glucose is the body's main source of fuel. However, keep in mind that it is much healthier to get our dose of sugar from fruits or dairy products to get more energy from natural sugar. Also, sugar makes us happy. It produces dopamine in our brain, and immediately our mood changes to one of joy. Our coffee with a little sugar will instantly change your mood and make you a little happier.

Add milk

We usually serve coffee with milk. Lots of milk! Based on steam, we make milk foam. From here, we reach a real art in terms of preparing a cup of coffee. We serve you coffee in various shapes made with the help of foamed milk: hearts, circles, even portraits! And finally, it is very good to drink milk because it is an excellent source of protein and is full of important nutrients such as calcium, phosphorus, B vitamins, potassium and vitamin D. It is also said that drinking milk can prevent bone fractures.

Serve a coffee with a good friend

Now that you have learned about the benefits of (our) coffee in combination with those of sugar and milk, we invite you to serve a cup of coffee with milk and sugar! Or just a simple coffee! It's your choice! And if you're still going to visit us, we recommend that you take a good friend with you because we promise that you'll have a quiet, comfortable, cozy place to serve your coffee together.
